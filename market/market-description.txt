<b>Have your NewBulls always with you, in your pocket!</b> You pay by quickly scanning a QR code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. NewBull Wallet is a reference implementation of the "Simplified Payment Verification" as described in the NewBull whitepaper.


<b>FEATURES</b>

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is org-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of NewBull amount in NB, mNB and µNB.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of NewBull via NFC, QR codes or NewBull URLs.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
• App widget for NewBull balance.
• Safety: Supports SegWit and the new bech32 format.
• Privacy: Supports Tor via the separate Orbot app.
